Review:Slow Braised Lamb Shanks It'will blow your mind

Slow braised lamb shanks with a redwine sauce Comforting food. Rating : 10 stars

Lamb shank is a tough cut from the lamb leg that becomes tender and juicy with slow and low cooking. The foreshank comes from the front legs and is smaller than the hind shank, which comes from the back legs and is much meatier. As with all hard-working muscles, lamb shank is full of connective tissue and collagen
